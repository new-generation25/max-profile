Biography

About Tanya

Tanya Kostadinova is a Bulgarian-born international actress and filmmaker, currently based in Sofia and London. She began her journey with a BA in Drama Theatre from New Bulgarian University and refined her skills in Film Acting at London's Met Film School, Ealing Studios.

Recently, she earned a Master's Degree in Filmmaking and directed her first short film, "The Unbeliever." Tanya holds UK work permission and a European passport.
